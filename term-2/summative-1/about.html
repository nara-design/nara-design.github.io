Our story and vision

Luminary is making waves in the fashion world with its sustainable and ethically produced products. From stylish t-shirts and hoodies to trendy joggers and sneakers, Luminary offers the perfect balance of comfort, fashion, and sustainability. Every item is made with premium-quality organic fabrics and recycled materials, ensuring that the brand is eco-friendly and socially responsible. Luminary means a person of prominence or brilliant achievement, and a body of light. Those who wear the luminary brand are luminaries themselves, always striving for the best and one step ahead.

What sets Luminary apart from other streetwear brands is that it provides luxury for less. Despite being a sub-brand of a luxury leather goods label, Luminary's products are affordably priced, making it accessible to a wider audience. With its unique combination of style, sustainability, and affordability, Luminary has become a go-to choice for conscious fashionistas who want to make a statement while minimizing their impact on the environment. Whether you're out running errands or hanging out with friends, Luminary's streetwear collection will have you looking and feeling good.
